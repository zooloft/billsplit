Following user self-reporting in a sheet, tell each person in household what they owe from their portion of the groceries box order, including proportional share of delivery fee. (Default delivery fee $4.99, but make adjustable) Share of delivery fee is set by $ value of items ordered.

Ingest data in the form of a .csv file, data structure: PersonName, OrderItem, UnitQuantity, UnitListPrice

Display reported order total to reconcile with actual bill (to highlight case in which items were actually ordered but not self-reported)

Option to apply discount on all list prices, default to 33% (does not apply to delivery fee)

Option to add sales tax, for users in locations where sales tax applies to food (tax does not apply to delivery fee)

Option of free delivery above certain order $ threshold (calculated based on total price after any discounts, not including delivery fee)
